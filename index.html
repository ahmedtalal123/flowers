Essential parts of Flowers:

The essential parts of a flower can be considered in two parts, the vegetative part, consisting of petals and associated structures in the perianth, and the reproductive or sexual parts.
A typical flower consists of four kinds of structures attached to the tip of a short stalk, which are arranged in a whorl. four main whorls are calyx, corolla, androecium and gynaecium.
